SOMETHING ABOUT US

You Can is an organization that was born to achieve a match between hearts... We walk through life, looking to fall in love, have a partner or companion of life and adventures, someone to take care of and who takes care of us, a confidant and above all someone to whom we can give our love unconditionally and feel that all that love returns transformed ... Shall I tell you something ? They are looking for the same kind of love ... At You Can we not only rescue dogs that are unprotected, sad and scared on the street, but we also take care of them, teach them, give them love and prepare them so that when "THE MATCH" arrives they are ready to go somewhere that they can call home ... At You Can we invite you to remember the candor, the hug, the tenderness, the amazement, the innocence, the look, the patience, the delivery without fear ... the confidence of when you were children. And we can't find a better way for you to do it, than by giving a home and love to someone who will never give you back less than what you give...
