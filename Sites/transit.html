EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW TO FOSTER A DOG

Having a dog in transit means give a home to those who were rescued and are waiting to be adopted. It is basically to care for them until they find a family. As a transients, you will assume the responsibilities of sanitation that the pet requires for a period that usually does not extend more than two weeks.
It should be noted that You Can or projects cover all expenses that may arise, be it food, veterinarians, medication or deworming. In addition, there is a system of volunteers for the transfer in case You cannot attend or stay for a consultation or surgical procedure.
